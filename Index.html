    Samosa

    What is Samosa?

    A Samosa is a fried/baked pastry with a savory filling, such as spiced potatoes, peas, and peanuts.
    It is enjoyed with chilli dipping as a snack or even as a main course.
    We will be selling delicious home-cooked samosas made with authentic spices and love!


    Momo

    What is Momo?

    Momo is the heart of Nepalese cuisine.
    It is a Nepali-Tibetan steamed dumpling made of all-purpose flour and filled with either meat or vegetables.
    It is prepared with special spices which add unique taste to suit the Nepalese palate.



    Rasmalai

    What is Rasmalai?

    Ras Malai is a mix of soft Rabsari, dipped and soaked in sweet Rabri.
    Rasbari is spongy cottage cheese ball, made with milk, lemon juice, and sugar
    Rabri is a silky smooth cream made with milk, condense cream, cardamom, almonds, pistachios, cashews, saffron, and sugar
